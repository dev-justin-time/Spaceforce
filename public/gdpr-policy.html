EU GDPR Compliance Report

Adherence to the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679.

1. Data Minimization ARTICLE 5

Spaceforce Squadron adheres to strict data minimization principles. We only process the minimal personal identifiers (Username, Avatar) required to facilitate the neural-link multiplayer simulation.

2. Right to Erasure ARTICLE 17

Pilots have the "Right to be Forgotten." All mission logs, inventory state, and ledger transactions associated with a pilot's identity can be requested for permanent termination via the Coalition Database interface.

3. Data Portability ARTICLE 20

Pilot dossiers (User Stats, High Scores, Inventory) are stored in an interoperable format within the WebsimSocket collection framework, allowing for theoretical data portability between compatible Coalition nodes.

4. Cross-Border Transfers ARTICLE 44

Data is transmitted via encrypted sub-space channels (WSS/HTTPS). While the simulation operates on a global scale, all regional data handling is governed by standard contractual clauses ensuring a high level of protection regardless of the pilot's physical sector.
