## 1. MISSION DEPLOYMENT HUB (THE HANGAR)
The Hangar serves as the central command for all United Terran Coalition (UTC) orbital operations.

### 1.1 Sector Operations
Deployment is divided into distinct stellar sectors, each with unique environmental hazards:
*   **Frontier:** Low-threat patrol zones (e.g., Sector Alpha). Ideal for basic flight certification.
*   **Deep Space:** High-density asteroid fields and frozen battlefields (Crystal Expanse).
*   **The Void:** Extremely hostile environments near gravitational anomalies (Event Horizon).
*   **Experimental/Black Ops:** Restricted zones testing UTC prototypes (Cyber Core, Omega Protocol).
*   **Ancient Ruins:** Sectors containing Precursor artifacts (Temple of Sol).

### 1.2 Gameplay Modes
*   **Missions:** Standard objective-based sorties against automated or rogue targets.
*   **Team Ops:** Multi-pilot cooperative engagements synced via neural-link (Websim Socket).
*   **PvP Skirmish:** Direct live-fire training against other active Coalition pilots.

## 2. SPACEFORGE STARSHIP DESIGNER
The Spaceforge is a modular engineering laboratory for starship configuration.

### 2.1 Hull Classifications
*   **Interceptor (Default):** Balanced speed, shielding, and firepower. High maneuverability.
*   **Bomber (Heavy):** +50 Shielding, +20% Damage, -20% Speed. Best for capital-scale strikes.
*   **Scout (Stealth):** +30% Speed, -20% Shielding. Narrow profile, high evade potential.

### 2.2 Modular Slots
A pilot can equip up to 9 specialized systems:
*   **Core Processing:** Drives the "Computing" stat. Affects fire rates and processing efficiency.
*   **Ammunition:** Swaps primary fire modes (Plasma = +Damage, Ion = -Enemy Speed, Vortex = Area Damage).
*   **Propulsion:** Determines max speed and lateral "curve" handling.
*   **Defensive/Shielding:** Higher capacity requires more energy draw.
*   **Subsystems (A/B):** Stackable upgrades like Neutron Plating or Quantum Reality Anchors.

### 2.3 The "Modular Override" Nuance
Activating the **Modular Override** (Purple Toggle) bypasses standard safety limiters.
*   **Benefit:** Massive boosts to Damage (+40%) and Speed (+20%).
*   **Risk:** Structural integrity becomes "STRESSED," Energy Drain spikes (+250%), and Shielding drops (-20%). Use only for critical endgame pushes.

## 3. POLITICAL LEDGER & DIPLOMACY
The simulation features a dynamic reputation system with five primary factions.

### 3.1 Reputation Standings
*   **SpaceForce:** Gain standing by completing sorties and collecting loot in inner sectors.
*   **Helix Syndicate:** Corporate-aligned. Gain standing via market trade and high-tech acquisitions.
*   **The Crimson Void:** Pirate-aligned. High notoriety increases sell prices on the Black Market.
*   **Deep Space Intelligence (DSI):** Gain standing by discovering classified data or neutralizing specific alien threats.

### 3.2 Political Hit Squads
If your standing with a faction drops below **-60 (Hostile)**, there is a random chance during any sortie that a 3-ship **Hit Squad** will deploy to intercept you. Surviving these earns massive bonus scores and "Hostile Survival" medals.

## 4. INTERGALACTIC MARKET (DEPOT)
The Depot is a real-time marketplace where pilots buy and list hardware. Market state and listings are persisted in the Websim collections so listings, transactions and inventory survive sessions and sync across pilots.

### 4.1 "Uni" Economy
*   **Acquisition:** Earned through Mission Rewards, session scores, or collecting loot coins in-flight.
*   **Persistence:** All account balances, inventory items, market listings and transactions are stored in the project's Websim collections (e.g., accounts, inventory, market_listings_v2, transactions) so your assets and listings remain across reconnects and devices.
*   **The Accountant AI ("Ledger"):** A sentient LLM system that monitors your transaction history. It will provide dry, often judgmental feedback on your spending habits in the Hangar footer.
*   **Black Market Nuance:** Prices fluctuate based on "Classified" status. TOP SECRET items from Ancient sectors can cost 4x-10x more than standard hardware.

## 5. COALITION CODEX & XENOLOGY
Archival data on non-human entities encountered during the simulation.

### 5.1 Threat Profiles
*   **Greys:** Highly advanced UFOs with auto-aim laser bursts. High health.
*   **Reptilians:** Aggressive, scaly pilots that utilize high-speed ramming and erratic strafing.
*   **Predators (Yautja):** Bio-mechanical ships equipped with **Active Cloaking**. They are nearly invisible until they fire their plasma casters.
*   **Anunnaki:** Massive gold-clad precursors. Slow fire rate but devastating kinetic damage.

## 6. SYSTEM CONFIGURATION & TECH
### 6.1 Input Nuances
*   **Gyroscope:** Recommended for mobile pilots for intuitive banking. Requires explicit gesture to sync.
*   **Mouse Aim:** Desktop only. Uses a "P-Controller" logic to drive the ship toward the cursor within world bounds.
*   **Virtual Pad:** Dual-joystick overlay for touch-screen sorties.

### 6.2 Visual Overlays
*   **CRT Simulation:** Toggles scanlines and pixel-grid effects to simulate a retro-futuristic cockpit terminal.
*   **Bloom FX:** Enhances the glow of plasma tracers and engine nozzles. Can be disabled to improve FPS on low-power devices.
